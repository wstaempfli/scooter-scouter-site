What it does

Scooter Scouter shows live, rentable e-scooters and e-bikes from across Swiss operators on a single map. Spot the nearest available ride, see its battery and range, and tap to open the operator's own app to unlock and go. It's free, has no account, and doesn't track you.

Independent app

Scooter Scouter is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any mobility operator. All operator names and brand colours are trademarks of their respective owners. Vehicle data is provided via public open-mobility feeds, including the Open Data Platform Mobility Switzerland (opentransportdata.swiss).
