Common questions

Why don't I see my location? Scooter Scout needs location permission to show your position and the nearest vehicles. You can enable it in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Scooter Scout.

Why is a vehicle's battery or availability wrong? Vehicle data comes from the operators' public live feeds and is shown as-is; it can occasionally be out of date.

How do I rent? Tap a vehicle, then “open” — Scooter Scout hands you off to that operator's own app, where you unlock and pay.
