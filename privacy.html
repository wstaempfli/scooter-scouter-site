Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Overview

Scooter Scout is a free iOS app that shows live, rentable e-scooters and e-bikes from many shared-mobility operators across Switzerland on a single map. When you find a vehicle you want, you tap “open” and Scooter Scout hands you off to that operator's own app, where you rent and pay.

The short version: Scooter Scout does not have user accounts, does not run analytics or advertising, does not use tracking, and does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data about you. We have no servers that receive data from your phone, because the app does not send us anything.

How your location is used

If you grant location permission, your location is used only on your device to show your position on the map, find and sort the vehicles nearest to you, and calculate distances. Your location never leaves your device for our purposes — we do not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell it, and there is no Scooter Scout server or database that receives it. If you decline location permission, the app still works; you simply won't see your own position or distance-based sorting.

No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads

  • No accounts. No sign-up, no profile, no name/email/phone.
  • No analytics. No usage-measurement tools.
  • No tracking. No advertising or tracking technologies, no advertising identifiers, no cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • No advertising. The app shows no ads.
  • No personal data retained, on your device or anywhere else.

Where the vehicle data comes from

The vehicle and station information comes from public, third-party data sources, including the sharedmobility.ch aggregator (provided through Switzerland's open mobility data platform) and the individual operators surfaced in the app (for example Lime, Voi, Bolt, Bird, Dott, nextbike, Donkey, PickeBike, and PubliBike/Velospot). The app requests this public data — vehicle positions, types, and battery levels — directly from those sources. This is information about the vehicles, not about you; the requests include no personal information about you and are not used to identify you. The data is shown as-is and may not always be accurate, complete, current, or available. Maps are displayed using Apple Maps.

Opening an operator's app

When you tap “open,” Scooter Scout hands off to the operator's own app or website. From that point you're interacting with the operator, not with Scooter Scouter, and anything you do there — accounts, location, payments — is governed by that operator's own privacy policy and terms.

Children's privacy

Scooter Scout does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the effective date above and post the updated policy here.
